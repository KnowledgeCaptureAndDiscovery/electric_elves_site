Scope of the Resulting System

  • Support ìdeploymentsî of teams for short-term ìactionsî

  • Involves both people and computers

  • Team is fully ìagentizedî with proxies for people

  • Supports dynamic integration of agents created by others

    Detail

  • Predefined templates for such deployments

  • Mixed-initiative, distributed plan refinement process

  • Includes selection of team members and computing resources

  • Monitor deployment of both humans and computers

  • Coordinate response to a crisis
