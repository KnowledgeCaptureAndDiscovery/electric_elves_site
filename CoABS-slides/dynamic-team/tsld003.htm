Motivating Example

    A critical demo is scheduled at the last minute and you must determine who will go, plan to get equipment there or arrange for the rental, plan the travel, and monitor the plan.

  • Plan Demo in Washington:

    • Schedule and execute a meeting to determine who is going
    • Plan the travel arrangements for participants
    • Plan and execute the shipping of equipment

  • Participant becomes ill:

    • Select and recruit qualified replacement

  • Flight is delayed:

    • Notify appropriate parties and reschedule meetings

  • Equipment is delayed:

    • Locate and arrange local computer rentals
