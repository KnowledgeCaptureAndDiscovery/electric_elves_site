Issues in Task-Based Matching (II)

  • Requesters will not provide exact description of required capability

    e.g.: find route to San Diego

    • Missing input data: from where?
    • Imprecise specs: surface route? air route?
    • Qualification of results expected: 3ft segments? major points?

  • Third parties may need to be invoked to help specify all inputs needed

    e.g.: find route from LA to San Diego

    • Route planner agent needs a map as an input
    • Route planner agent takes lat/long as input, not city names
