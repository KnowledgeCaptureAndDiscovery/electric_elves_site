Issues in Task-Based Matching (I)

  • A single agent can perform a wide range of tasks

    • Currently, agents can do at most a handful (i.e., one)
    • Nominate alternative agents

  • Flexible invocation

    • A request to ìRegister for local conferenceî is treated by a PA as ìArrange travel to a meetingî

  • Invocations of other agents

    • Advertise delegation to other agents, consultations to get additional information

  • Describing peopleís capabilities

    • Project assistants as ìeverything agentsî (information agents, matching services, proxies of travel agents, etc.)
